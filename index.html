The Coffee Of Success

A good coffee starts from its beans. And at U-ories, we use Robusta beans for their distinctively strong taste note and it is also the symbol of Vietnamese Coffee.

With 2X more caffeine than other beans, Barxiu Coffee will boost your productivity, keep you focus and "in the zone" to chase all your deadlines away 😊.

Strong, Bold & Flavorful

As Robusta beans have 2 times more caffeine than other beans, they are also low in acidity and without the sour aftertaste.

That is how Vietnamese Coffee at Barxiu can have more coffee than milk, giving a Strong, Bold and Flavorful taste that other coffee doesn't have.

Bac Xiu- "Liquid Tiramisu"?

Bac Xiu is a traditional coffee in Vietnam, which was created to serve people who cannot take the bitterness of coffee.

It is sweet & creamy, with a little touch of coffee, and is often referred to as "liquid tiramisu" - which will serve as a delightful drink to anyone.

Ca Phe Sua - Vietnamese Coffee with Milk

This is the symbol of Vietnamese Coffee - "Ca Phe" means coffee and "Sua" means milk.

Known for its Strong & Bold coffee taste, with 2/3 of the drink is coffee and a little bit of milk, this will give you the perfect coffee kick whenever you need to get into your "focus mode" 😊.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my coffee?

Your coffee will be delivered tomorrow (between 12PM to 5PM) when you place an order today before 5PM.

If you place an order after 5PM, your coffee will be delivered on the day after tomorrow

If you are not available to receive your order tomorrow, please chat with our friendly support staffs using the "Ask Us" button located at the bottom right corner of this page 😊.

How long can I keep the coffee?

As for our product expiry date, our coffee would last at least 5 days if kept in the fridge (between standard temperature of 0-4 degree).

As we don't use any preservatives in our products, we recommend customers to consume within that timeframe.

What is the best way to enjoy Vietnamese Coffee?

We recommend adding ice - a lot of ice - to get the best taste of Vietnamese Coffee.

As the coffee is rich and bold, the ice will help to balance the taste, and that's the best way to enjoy our coffee.

What do I do with the empty bottles?

As part of our effort to keep the Earth clean, we do have a recycle program where you return 10 empty bottles to get 1 free bottle (same size) in your next order. So please keep the used bottles and let us know when you want to return them 😊!

How much is the delivery fee?

Our islandwide delivery fee is $6. But you'll get FREE Delivery with orders from $20.

Is there a self-collection option?

Yes, we also offer self-collection at 183 Jalan Pelikat, B1-119, S537643 - between 10:30AM to 1PM daily.

Just simply select "Pick Up" under "Delivery method" section on our Check Out page, and you don't have to pay for any delivery fee 😊.
